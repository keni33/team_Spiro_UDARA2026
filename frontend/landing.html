The Flow

From site photo to
actionable risk score

Three steps. Under 90 seconds end-to-end. No engineering degree required.

01
📸

Capture & Upload

Site supervisor photographs columns, foundation, or rebar at a defined construction milestone. Upload up to 5 images directly from their phone.

02
🤖

AI Risk Analysis

Claude Vision analyses each image for 8 categories of structural risk — rebar spacing, block quality, foundation irregularity, crack patterns, and PPE compliance.

03
📋

Score + PDF Report

Instant risk score (SAFE → CRITICAL) with plain-English explanation. One-click PDF inspection report for insurers, architects, and regulators.

Risk Classification

Four levels. Clear actions for each.

SAFE

No significant structural concerns. Continue construction per approved plan.

MONITOR

Minor concerns detected. Document and re-inspect at next milestone.

HIGH RISK

Significant issues found. Notify architect and inspector before continuing work.

CRITICAL

Stop work immediately. LASBCA notified automatically. Structural engineer required on site.
